The Meaning Behind Exera

"Xera" comes from "Zero" — a reflection of our humble beginnings. We started with no substantial financial backing, no advantages. This taught us to build something meaningful from nothing, and instilled a work ethic that drives everything we do.

The "exe" pays homage to the nostalgic .exe executable file extension — remember running those setup.exe files? It represents our deep-rooted passion for technology and IT since the early days.

Our logo — a struck-through infinity symbol — carries a powerful message: "Nothing lasts forever." It's our constant reminder to stay mindful of relationships and surroundings, to never take people for granted despite how hard we work.

Nothing lasts forever — stay humble, value every connection.

Proudly Made in Moris

Sustainability Champions 2025

Our Softwares are Made in Moris Certified

The Made in Moris label is a quality certification program by the Association of Mauritian Manufacturers (AMM), certifying that our product originates from Mauritius with at least 80% of production on Mauritian soil. This certification is achieved with the support of SGS, the world's leading inspection and verification company.

We're honored to have been named Sustainability Champions in our Made in Moris cohort — a recognition of our commitment to eco-friendly software solutions and sustainable business practices.
