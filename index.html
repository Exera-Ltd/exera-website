From zero.

No backing. No advantages. No safety net. Just a stubborn belief that you can build something that matters out of nothing.

“Xera” comes from zero — where we began. The “exe” is a wink to the old .exe files we grew up running — the day we fell for this whole computing thing and never looked back.

Our logo is an infinity with a line through it: nothing lasts forever. It keeps us honest — never take a client, a teammate, or a good day for granted. Stay low. Stay humble. Build like it counts.
