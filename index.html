Our Story

Built from zero.

"Xera" comes from zero — a nod to our humble beginnings. No backing, no advantages. It taught us to build something meaningful from nothing, and it shaped the work ethic behind everything we do.

The "exe" pays homage to the nostalgic .exe file — remember running those setup files? It captures our lifelong love of technology.

Our logo — a struck-through infinity — carries one message: nothing lasts forever. A reminder to stay humble and never take people or connections for granted.
